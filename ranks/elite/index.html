Elite

The Elite logo as a square image

This page is about the Elite rank.

Commands

Everything in VIP plus:

/cartographytable

Aliases: /carttable
Description: Opens a cartography table at any point.

/smithingtable

Aliases: /smithtable
Description: Opens a smithing table at any point.

Perks

Other non-command perks of Elite are:

  • Creation of up to 7 homes from the normal 2
  • Creation of up to 5 warps from the normal 0
  • Changing the colour of your name in chat
  • The [Elite] tag in chat
  • The [Elite] tag next to your name
  • Custom join message
