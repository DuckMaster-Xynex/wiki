Commands

/balance

Aliases: /bal /money
Description: Shows your amount of X Coins.

/pay [player] [amount]

Aliases: /epay
Description: Pays the specified player the noted amount of X Coins.

/sell

Aliases: /esell
Description: Sells your held item for it's worth in X Coins.
